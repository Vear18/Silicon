Is any of my personal information stored in the App?
Yes, the app stores some personal information, including your name, address, and email, for account management purposes.
What formats can I download my transaction history in?
You can download your transaction history in various formats such as PDF and CSV.
Can I schedule future transfers?
Yes, you can schedule future transfers using the app. Just go to the Transfers section and select the date and amount you'd like to transfer.
When can I use Banking App services?
You can use the Banking App services 24/7. However, transfers might take up to 1-3 business days depending on the type of transaction.
Can I create my own password that is easy for me to remember?
Yes, the app allows you to create a custom password, as long as it meets the security requirements.
What happens if I forget or lose my password?
If you forget your password, you can reset it through the "Forgot Password" feature in the login section.
